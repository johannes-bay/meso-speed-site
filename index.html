Design

One number on glass.

A liquid glass disk. Your speed in the centre, GPS-accurate, updated every second. The arc underneath fills as you approach the speed limit — diffused through the glass so you feel it before you read it. Amber when close. Red when over.

Themes

Five modes of seeing.

Slide to switch. Dark for night driving. Light for daylight. HUD for windshield projection. Night preserves your dark adaptation. Dusk for the hours in between.
