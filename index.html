Support
The speed reads zero even though I'm moving. Make sure location permissions are set to "While Using" or "Always". Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > Meso Speed.
GPS takes a long time to lock. A cold lock can take 10-30 seconds, especially indoors or in urban canyons. Move to an area with clear sky view. The GPS ring fills as signal improves.
Speed limits aren't showing. Speed limits are pulled from OpenStreetMap and require a network connection. Not all roads have speed limit data. You can set a manual limit in Settings.
How do I use HUD mode? Tap the rectangle icon (bottom right). Place your phone face-up under the windshield. The display mirrors and turns red for night visibility.
Does the app track me? No. Meso Speed has no analytics, no accounts, no servers. All data stays on your device. Read the full privacy policy.
